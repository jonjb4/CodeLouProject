Leadership of the Church

Highland United Methodist Church is part of the international United Methodist Church denomination and its governance is guided by the United Methodist Book of Discipline, which is reviewed once every four years at the General Conference. In particular, Highland UMC is part of the Louisville District of the Kentucky Conference. The pastoral staff and church staff work closely with the appointed lay leaders of the church who are organized into various committees to ensure the smooth functioning of the church.

We have the privilege of being led by dedicated men and women of God who serve selflessly and sacrificially that flows out of their love for God and people.

Administrative Council

The church council is made up of the various ministry chairpersons and is charged with leading the church through visioning, setting goals and evaluating the effectiveness of its various ministries.

The trustees are charged with maintaining all church property, approving its use and ensuring that it is utilized safely.

Finance Committee

The finance committee is charged with the fiduciary responsibilities of the church such as preparing an annual budget to support the ministries of the church and ensuring that the church operates within budget.
