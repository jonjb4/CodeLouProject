Here are some ways you can grow together with other members of our church family as disciples of Christ.

INDUCTIVE BIBLE STUDY

We continue to meet every 2nd and 4th Thursdays @ 5.30pm in the church office. We will be using Dr. David Thompson's "Bible Study that Works" as our guiding text as we consider how we can study the BIble inductively so that it is allowed to speak to us on its own terms.

Choir

UNITED METHODIST WOMEN (UMW)

The UMW has a long and storied history of supporting missions in the world through serving, and we would love to have you join us in this work at HUMC. The circle meets once a quarter, so be sure to check our Church News page for upcoming meetings and events.

THE EXPLORERS
