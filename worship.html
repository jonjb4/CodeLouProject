Worship at Highland UMC

We worship as a church at 10:50 a.m. every Sunday. We love to sing hymns of praise and to read the Psalms responsively.

The preaching at Highland UMC is Christ-centered and recognizes that the living Word of God can deform, reform and transform us through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Sermons seek to challenge the listener to apply Biblical truths practically in daily living and to perservere as a disciple of Christ. In recognition of our membership with the church universal, we typically preach from the lectionary during the Lenten and Advent seasons.

We celebrate Holy Communion every week as the center-piece of worship, which allows us to proclaim with one voice the mystery of our faith "Christ has died, Christ is risen, Christ will come again!"

Childcare is available during the worship service but children are strongly encouraged to participate and worship together with their families and with the larger church family.

Prayer Meeting

We gather at 5:30pm every Wednesday for a mid-week time of renewal and contemplation as we pray and seek after God's direction for our church and our families.
